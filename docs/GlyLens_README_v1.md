# GlyLens
## Food Intelligence for Better Decisions

GlyLens is an evidence-based Food Intelligence Platform that helps people understand the glycemic impact of food through transparent, explainable, and trusted intelligence.

## Mission

Help users answer:

- What am I eating?
- What is the likely glycemic impact?
- Why?
- What evidence supports it?
- What are better alternatives?

## Core Principles

- Data > AI
- Evidence > Opinion
- Explainability First
- Security by Design
- Cost Optimization First
- Ingredient-First Intelligence
