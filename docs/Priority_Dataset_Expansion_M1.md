## 4. Expansion Notes

- This package is intentionally conservative: numeric values are only recorded when supported by existing authoritative source categories.
- Many new items are candidate additions with primary evidence expected from IFCT, NIN, USDA FoodData Central, or FAO/WHO GI tables.
- Diet cola items are recorded as zero glycemic load because they contain no available carbohydrates according to product formulation.
- Product-level values for generic categories such as `Sugar Free Biscuits`, `Diabetic Atta`, and `Protein Bars` remain unavailable until specific brand-level evidence is acquired.
- This expansion is structured to preserve the GlyLens evidence-first corpus discipline and to maintain priority focus on India and diabetes-relevant items.
